While researching UPI fraud, I was surprised to learn how easily people can be manipulated into sharing sensitive
information due to panic or lack of awareness. Many victims trust callers who pretend to be bank officials or
customer support executives, without verifying their identity. I also realized that a common misconception is 
that entering a UPI PIN is required to receive money, which is not true.
This task made me more aware of the importance of digital security in everyday life. One habit I will change is 
being more cautious while dealing with unknown calls, messages, or payment requests. I will always verify the 
authenticity of any communication before taking action. Overall, this activity helped me understand the importance 
of staying informed, alert, and responsible while using digital platforms to prevent cybercrime.
