Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment or collect requests
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions
- Use only official and trusted apps for digital payments

UPI Safety Tips:
- Remember that receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN
- Double-check the receiver’s details before sending money

Reporting:
- Report cyber fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number: 1930 immediately in case of fraud
