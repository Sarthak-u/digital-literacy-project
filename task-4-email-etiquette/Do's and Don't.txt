DO’s:
- Maintain a clean and professional online profile
- Think carefully before posting any content
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Respect others and communicate politely online
- Verify the authenticity of information before sharing

DON’Ts:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or financial details
